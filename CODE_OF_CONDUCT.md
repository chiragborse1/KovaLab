# Code of Conduct

Kova should be a useful, serious, and welcoming open source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections quickly.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the work.
- Share enough context for others to reproduce your problem.
- Be careful with logs, screenshots, tokens, personal data, and private chats.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Posting another person's private information.
- Publishing secrets, exploits, or security-sensitive proof in public threads.
- Using the project spaces for spam, scams, or unrelated promotion.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content; ask someone to stop; or
block a person from project spaces when needed to protect the community.
